Child Welfare Initiatives

GIVE NEPAL is committed to promoting child rights and ensuring the well-being of every child in our community. We believe that every child deserves to live in a safe, supportive environment where their rights are protected and their voices are heard.

Through our various initiatives, we work tirelessly to advocate for children's rights, provide support to vulnerable children, and empower them to reach their full potential.

  • Advocating for child rights at local, national, and international levels
  • Providing legal assistance and support to children in need
  • Offering counseling and rehabilitation services to children affected by abuse, neglect, or exploitation
  • Empowering children to become agents of change in their communities
  • Organizing awareness campaigns to educate communities about child rights and protection
  • Collaborating with government agencies, NGOs, and community organizations to address issues affecting children
  • Conducting research and surveys to identify areas of need and develop targeted interventions
  • Provide scholarships and financial assistance to underprivileged children
  • Establish schools and learning centers in remote and underserved areas
  • Offer educational materials, resources, and support to students and teachers

Stories of Hope

From Street Child to Successful Entrepreneur

Meet Raju, who once lived on the streets, struggling to survive. With the support of our NGO, Raju received education and vocational training. Today, he runs his own successful business and is a role model for other children.

A New Beginning for Maya

Maya's childhood was filled with hardship and abuse. But with the help of our counseling and rehabilitation services, Maya found the strength to overcome her past traumas. Now, she's pursuing her dreams of becoming a nurse and giving back to her community.

Empowering Girls Through Education

In a society where girls are often overlooked, our NGO is working to change the narrative. By providing scholarships and educational opportunities to girls, we're empowering them to break barriers and pursue their aspirations.

Rebuilding Lives After Disaster

When disaster struck our community, many families lost everything. But through our relief efforts and rebuilding initiatives, we're helping families rebuild their lives and create a brighter future for their children.
